## Safe Cooking Temperature Guidelines (UK FSA)

For reference, the UK Food Standards Agency recommends these minimum internal temperatures:

- **Poultry (chicken, turkey)**: 75°C
- **Pork**: 75°C
- **Beef/Lamb (medium)**: 63°C
- **Beef/Lamb (well done)**: 71°C
- **Fish**: 63°C
- **Reheating food**: 75°C

**ALWAYS use a food thermometer to verify these temperatures are reached.**
